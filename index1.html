Local Junior Competition

Registration 2013

Registrations for the Local Junior Competition will be held in March at the Canteen at No. 2 Oval Leeton from 12 to 2 pm. This year we will be registering online through the FFA's registraion site www.myfootballclub.com.au . Registrations for the local competition open 2nd of Febuary. Players can also be registered at Felix Joinery during business hours.

Teams

The club has the following teams play each Saturday afternoon from 12 noon till 3pm.
Under 5
Under 6
Under 7
Under 8
Under 9
B Grade (Under 10, 11)
A Grade (Under 12, 13, 14, 15) (A Grade also allows girls up to under 16 to play – ie. born 1996)
Depending on numbers in each of the age groups, at least 4 teams will be formed. If there are insufficient numbers in a particular age group, it maybe combined with another. When registrations are finalised, teams will be drawn up and posted on the official website.
The club endeavours to make the teams as even as possible based on information from the previous years. This is not always possible due to other factors including player maturity, player absences etc. While the club will do its best to accommodate player preferences for team mates, depending on team numbers, players maybe asked to play in another team.

Competition

Competion will start in April 2013.
As soon as teams are drawn up, a draw will be made and posted on the official site. The club will attempt to play each age group at the same time each week.
