Leeton United Football Club

Summer Soccer

Results Week 2 31/10/2012

It was a great evening of soccer watched by an increasing number of fans. It was great to see some of the social players kick back, relax and watch the Opens' battle it out.

Open

The Open competition is still coming to terms with the smaller goals with plenty of near misses from long and close shots. The A Team looked dangerous the whole 40 minutes, but missed their top gun import Otis Barney when it came to putting it away. With the help of their Liverpool draft player, Manchester United should have won by more, missing plenty of opportunities in close.

The Gillenbah Dimmies raced away with their game as Pete's Team got on the pitch for the first time. The Dimmies are really getting into the swing of things with good displays of passing and support. Pete's Team struggled to get the ball in the net with plenty of shots going wide. Their fitness was also a factor as the battled to get back in defence late in the game.

Manchester United 3 (Andrew Luff, Braiden McInnes, Jason Bowe, Jake Single MVP) Def. The A Team 1 (Alex Blazevski 1 and MVP)

Gillenbah Dimmies 6 (Patrick Fondacaro, Luke Mandaglio, Anthony Trifogli, Nicholas Trifogli, Joseph Fondacaro 2 and MVP) Def. Pete's Team 1 (Peter Geltch-Kirkup, Daniel Berghofer MVP)
